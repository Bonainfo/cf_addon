Drip Mass Mailing

Mass mailing campaign alone won't convert your prospects into buyers and net-surfers to your blog readers if they do not get correct mail at the right time. But Drip mass mailing- now that's what's automated email marketing should be more like. Our Odoo Drip mass mailing lets you send pre-designed emails to your customers at scheduled times so you can entice their interest in your website.

Design exciting mail templates and set the timers so they reach out to your prospects at the exact time which you want. Nurture them into possible leads and then faithful customers. OR, retain your existing customers to keep those bills rollings.

Basic Features
  • This module helps in sending marketing information to prospects repeatedly over longer periods of time in order to nurture prospects or leads through the marketing funnel.
  • Enable/disable drip mass mailing of the mailing list.
  • Set drip mass mailing start and end date on mailing list.
  • Manage outgoing mail server based on mailing list.
  • Manage drip email templates.
  • Manage drip templates in mailing list .
  • Display Drip mailing history in mailing list as well as in contact.
